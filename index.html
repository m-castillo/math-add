1-Minute Addition Challenge

Instructions

Read before you start.

  • You have to add the numbers you see on the screen. You can use your spiral, a pencil and an eraser, or a whiteboard with a marker (make sure it is not a Sharpie!)
  • You will have 1 minute to solve as many problems you can. See the timer on the top section of the screen.
  • When the minute is over, you will see how many problems you solved correctly.
  • It does NOT matter how many you solve the first time. But you have to be better every time you play.
  • You can play with a friend, with your brother or sister, or even with your parents! Perhaps one of your teachers can play with you!
  • When you are ready, click on the button below and start playing. Have fun!
